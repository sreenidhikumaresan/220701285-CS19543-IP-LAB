RESEARCH CENTRE

The department is recognized as a Research Center for M.S / Ph.D by the ANNA UNIVERSITY. This recognition allows the department to offer advanced research opportunities and facilities for students and faculty members, fostering innovation and scholarly work in various fields of computer science and engineering.

AFFILIATION

The Department has PERMANENT AFFILIATION from ANNA UNIVERSITY. This permanent affiliation ensures that the department adheres to the high academic standards set by the university, providing a stable and recognized academic environment for students pursuing their undergraduate and postgraduate studies.

AICTE - CII SURVEY

The department is Ranked 3rd in the AICTE-CII Survey conducted Nationwide for Industry Institute Interaction. This ranking reflects the department's strong collaboration with industry partners, enhancing the practical learning experience for students and improving their employability through various industry-aligned programs and initiatives.

IIT BOMBAY REMOTE CENTER

The department acts as a Remote Center for conducting Spoken Tutorials, Workshops, and certifications by IIT BOMBAY in collaboration with ISTE and funded by MHRD. This collaboration provides students and faculty with access to high-quality resources and training from IIT Bombay, promoting continuous learning and skill development.

IBM CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE

The department is credited with IBM Software Centre for Excellence, and the lab is set for the same. This center provides students with the opportunity to work with the latest IBM software and tools, enhancing their technical skills and preparing them for careers in the technology industry through hands-on experience and specialized training.
